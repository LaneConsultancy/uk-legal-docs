Frequently Asked Questions

Are these documents legally valid?
Yes. Our documents are based on ICO guidelines, UK GDPR, PECR, and the Consumer Rights Act 2015. They're designed to help UK businesses meet their legal obligations. However, for complex situations or if you need legal advice specific to your circumstances, we recommend consulting a solicitor.
Do I need a privacy policy for my website?
If you collect any personal data (including via contact forms, analytics, cookies, or email signups), UK law requires you to have a privacy policy. The ICO can fine businesses up to ¬£17.5 million for serious breaches.
What about the cookie law?
PECR (Privacy and Electronic Communications Regulations) requires you to tell users about cookies and, for non-essential cookies, get their consent. Our cookie policy covers this. Note: you'll also need a cookie consent banner on your site ‚Äî we include guidance on this.
How is this different from free generators?
Free generators typically produce generic, one-size-fits-all documents that may not cover your specific situation or comply with current UK law. Our documents are tailored to your business type and activities, use plain English, and are kept up-to-date with legal changes.
